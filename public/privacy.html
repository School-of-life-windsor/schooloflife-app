Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 19, 2026

At School of Life (referred to as "Company," "we," "us," or "our"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data of our users, particularly scouts, parents, and camp leaders who use our scout portal and mobile application (the "Application" or "App").

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you use the Application, as well as your rights regarding your data, including the right to delete your account.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a functional and safe experience, we collect several categories of information, depending on how you interact with our platform:

A. Account and Profile Information

When you register for a School of Life account, we collect:

  • Usernames and Credentials: Your chosen username, email address, and secure password.
  • Profile Data: Your name, profile photo, bio, role (e.g., Scout, Parent, Leader), group/troop numbers, and badge progress.
  • Contact Information: Phone number and parent/guardian email address (for minor users).

B. User-Generated Content (UGC)

We collect any photos, text posts, chat messages, comments, files, and activity logs that you upload, submit, or transmit through the Application, including shared check-ins and camp updates.

C. Device and Usage Data

We automatically collect information from the device you use to access the Application, including:

  • IP address, operating system version, and device model.
  • Log files, feature usage metrics, and diagnostic info (crash logs).
  • Geolocation data (collected only with your explicit permission, typically during wilderness camps or check-ins).

2. How We Use Your Information

We process your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your user account and profile.
  • To facilitate coordination, communication, and badge tracking within your scout unit or camp group.
  • To organize, manage registrations, and ensure safety during physical wilderness camps.
  • To moderate content, enforce our EULA, and keep the platform free from objectionable content.
  • To respond to customer support inquiries and send system notifications.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We share information only under the following circumstances:

  • Within the Community: Your username, profile photo, and role are visible to other authorized members of your group or troop.
  • Service Providers: We may share data with third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf (e.g., secure cloud hosting, email delivery, database management) under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose data if required by law, subpoena, or to protect the safety of our users, campers, or the general public.

4. Children's Privacy

Because the Application is used by scouts, we are dedicated to protecting children's privacy. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Account registration for children under 13 must be initiated and approved by a verified parent or legal guardian. Parents have the right to review, edit, or request the deletion of their child's personal data at any time by contacting us.

5. Data Retention and Account Deletion Rights

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to provide the Services or as required by law. Under Apple App Store Guideline 5.1 and Google Play Store policies, you have the absolute right to request the deletion of your account and all associated personal information at any time.

How to Delete Your Account and Data:

  1. In-App Deletion: You can delete your account directly inside the Application by going to Settings > Account > Delete Account. Follow the prompts to confirm your decision. Your account will be disabled immediately and scheduled for permanent deletion.
  2. Email Request: You can submit a deletion request by sending an email from your registered email address to privacy@schooloflifeapp.com with the subject line "Request for Account Deletion."

What Happens When You Delete Your Account:

  • Your username, email address, profile photos, contact information, and progress history are permanently purged from our active databases.
  • Any User-Generated Content (UGC) you posted in public group forums may be anonymized or scrubbed of personal identifiers.
  • Backup copies of your data will be securely purged within thirty (30) days from our backup servers, in compliance with GDPR and standard privacy practices.

6. Information Security

We implement industry-standard administrative, physical, and technical security measures (such as SSL encryption during data transit) to safeguard your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage method is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or regulatory requirements. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy in the Application and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
